Link - http://localhost:8080/quotes
Usage - 1.All data in quotes page is populated from DB and data Trade-In price column keeps changing randomly every 10 seconds, Data is displayed using Websocket.
2.User can enter number of shares in 'Request Shares' column and click on subscribe button.
3.After calculations 'No Of Shares' will be deducted from Quotes pages and all share data will be added to User's page in My 'Portfolio option'
3.Similar process is followed when user sells their shares from my portfolio page.
